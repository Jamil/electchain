About
ElectChain's goal is to preserve, and advocate for the preservation of, information about our democracy for future generations, in a way that is censorship-resistant and easily verifiable.

This breaks down into two concrete tasks:
  1. Expose existing information that has been written to the blockchain, in an easy-to-consume way. People should be able to readily examine and read data. This comes in the form of the website you are on now, the ElectChain website. It aggregates information from decentralized sources, but (for now) is not decentralized itself.
  2. Upload new information to the blockchain from reputable sources. This comes in the form of the ElectChain smart contracts.
